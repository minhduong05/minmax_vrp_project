# Convergence experiments

Dung folder nay de luu metric hoi tu theo iteration hoac theo thoi gian.

Template CSV: `../templates/convergence.csv`.

Moi dong nen la mot checkpoint trong qua trinh chay:

- sau moi iteration;
- hoac sau moi khoang thoi gian;
- hoac moi khi co best objective moi.

Metric quan trong nhat la `best_max_route`, sau do den `best_total_distance` va `best_balance`.
